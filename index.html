A Tale of Two Souls

In my dreams, a voice keeps calling me forward. I see fragments - a young man's face aging in an instant, the sun whispering secrets I can't quite hear. These visions feel like memories, but whose memories are they?

From the towering peak of Zion to the endless Sea of Tears, from the glittering spires of Arcadia to the vast desert beyond - a truth lies scattered across this vast realm. And somewhere out there, Res waits. A being whose existence seems to echo through time itself, bound to me by forces I don't yet understand.

Dynamic Combat System

Every battle challenges you to master your weapons and adapt to survive. Pick up anything as a weapon and build momentum for powerful attacks. Know when to push forward and when to cool down - one wrong move can leave you disarmed and vulnerable. Build up momentum to unlock weapon skills, but overheat it and you risk losing your advantage.

  • Momentum: Weapons grow stronger with continuous use, unlocking special abilities
  • Adapt: Any object can become a weapon - each with unique attacks and skills
  • Emotions: Combat evolves based on companions' feelings and relationships
  • Charms: Customize fighting styles with magic enhancements and abilities

Meet Your Companions

Zein

A young man haunted by visions and memories of his mother, searching for answers about his connection to ancient powers.

Arika

A brave waitress from the local inn whose courage and quick thinking have saved Zein more than once. Her resourcefulness proves invaluable in combat.

Zell

Your lighthearted childhood friend whose humor brightens even the darkest moments. His unconventional thinking often leads to unexpected solutions.

Key Features

  • Seasonal Content: Experience a story that unfolds across multiple seasons
  • Environmental Puzzles: Work with your companions to solve ancient mysteries
  • Charm System: Customize your party's abilities through Benediction Charms
  • Dynamic AI: Companions make intelligent decisions based on their personalities
  • Rich World: Explore three distinct realms filled with secrets and mysteries

Three Realms to Explore

Mount Zion & Arcadia

A towering peak surrounded by a city of baroque architecture and ancient mysteries, where the remnants of Eden civilization still linger.

The Sea of Tears

Shallow waters stretch to the horizon, housing the Water City and countless secrets beneath its perpetual twilight surface.

The Desert Wastes

Endless dunes hide ancient mysteries and serve as home to the Sakojin, where survival demands adaptation.
